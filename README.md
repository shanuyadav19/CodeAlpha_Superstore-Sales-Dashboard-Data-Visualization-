# Superstore Sales Dashboard | Tableau

## Overview
This interactive Tableau dashboard analyzes Superstore sales performance using key business metrics and visualizations. It enables users to explore sales trends, customer segments, product categories, and profitability through interactive filters.
---

## Dashboard Features
- KPI Cards (Total Sales, Profit, Orders, Average Discount)
- Monthly Sales Trend
- Sales by Category
- Profit by Customer Segment
- Sales vs Profit Analysis
- Top 10 Most Profitable Products
- Interactive Region and Segment Filters
---

## Key Insights
- Technology generated the highest sales among all product categories.
- The Consumer segment contributed the highest overall profit.
- Sales showed strong growth during the final quarter, with November recording the highest sales.
- A small number of products contributed a significant share of total profit.
- Higher sales do not always result in higher profit, highlighting the importance of profit margin analysis.
